AI for Small Business Automation

Discover how artificial intelligence can transform your small business operations. Learn practical AI tools and strategies to automate repetitive tasks, improve customer service, and boost productivity without breaking the bank.

This comprehensive workshop covers essential AI automation tools specifically designed for small businesses...

This comprehensive workshop covers essential AI automation tools specifically designed for small businesses. You'll learn how to implement chatbots for customer service, automate email marketing campaigns, streamline inventory management, and use AI-powered analytics to make data-driven decisions.

Our expert instructors will guide you through hands-on exercises using popular AI platforms like Zapier, ChatGPT for business, and automated scheduling tools. By the end of this session, you'll have a clear roadmap for implementing AI solutions that can save you hours each week.

Perfect for entrepreneurs, small business owners, and managers looking to leverage AI technology without requiring technical expertise. All skill levels welcome - no coding experience necessary.
